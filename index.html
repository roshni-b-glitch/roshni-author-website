About the Author

Roshni Barua is a passionate storyteller who writes from the heart. Her stories are deeply inspired by real emotions, memories, love, growth, and the silent battles we all fight within. Through her words, she transforms personal experiences into relatable journeys that touch the soul. Writing for her is not just creativity — it is healing, expression, and connection.
